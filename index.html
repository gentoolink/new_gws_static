FAQ

Questions we hear a lot.

Will my customers know it's AI?
Mostly no. The AI texts like a real person — friendly, professional, answers their question. If someone asks "are you a robot?", it answers honestly. Most people never notice.
What if it gives wrong information?
We train it on your exact business info — hours, pricing, services, policies. If it doesn't know something, it says so and takes a message. No making things up. You can also set boundaries: "if it's an emergency, tell them to call 911 first."
How long does setup take?
3–5 days. We configure the AI with your business info, test it with real scenarios, and you're live. Not months of integration work.
What happens to my calls when you're closed?
Same as when we're open — the AI answers every call, 24/7. After-hours is actually where it earns the most. You wake up to booked appointments, not missed calls.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly subscription, no contract. If it's not working for you, we figure out why — or you cancel. No cancellation fees.
What phone number do my customers text?
Your existing number. We set up a Twilio number that routes to your AI, or we can port your current number over. Either way, your customers text the same number they've always had.
Do I need a smartphone?
No. All you need is a way to receive texts — and you already have that. Conversations go to your phone just like any other text. You can also get email summaries if you prefer.
How is this different from a voicemail?
Voicemails are passive — customers rarely leave them, and you can't respond to one in real time. The AI has a two-way conversation: it answers questions, books appointments, confirms details — right then while the customer is still interested.
