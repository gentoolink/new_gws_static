FAQ

Questions we hear a lot.

Do I need to know anything about websites?
Not a thing. If you can send an email, you can run your website. That's the whole point.
What if I need something unusual updated?
Just email it and describe what you want. If it's something we can do, we'll do it. If it's outside scope, we'll tell you and give you options.
How long does the initial build take?
48 hours from your onboarding call. We build it, send you a preview, you review, we refine — then it goes live.
What if I already have a website?
We can either rebuild it (common — a lot of existing sites aren't update-friendly) or assess whether it can be adapted. We'll be honest about which makes more sense after a free call.
Can I attach photos to my update emails?
Yes. Attach photos, CC your manager, write as much or as little as you need. It all goes to the same email address.
Is there a contract?
No contract. Monthly subscription, cancel anytime. The setup fee covers the build — the monthly keeps your site live and updated.
Do I get my own domain name?
Yes. We set up your domain (or move your existing one over) as part of the setup. It's your site — you own it.
How fast is "under 20 minutes"?
Most updates process in well under 20 minutes. You'll get a confirmation email the moment it's live showing exactly what changed.
