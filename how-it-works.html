Frequently Asked Questions

How does this actually help my business?

Ken starts with a free AI Audit — a 30-minute conversation where he listens to what's slowing you down and identifies where AI could realistically help. Then he builds the right tools for your situation: a chatbot that answers customer questions 24/7, automations that follow up with leads so they don't go cold, or a website that actually brings in calls. You stay focused on your work while the tech handles the repetitive stuff.

Can you manage my Google Business Profile?

Absolutely. We'll optimize and update your listing so you show up higher in local results and make a great first impression.

Can you set up an AI voice answering service for my business?

Yes! We set up and support AI voice answering services using proven third-party platforms. This helps you manage calls and book jobs even after hours—but you're always in control. (Just so you know: we don't build or own the software, we help you get set up and working.)

What if I want to answer my own calls?

That's totally up to you. Our solutions are flexible—use as much or as little automation as fits your business.

Do I have to rebuild my website?

No need, unless you want to. If you have a website you like, we'll work with it. If not, we can build a simple, effective site that's designed to bring in leads.

How much does it cost?

Our pricing is simple and upfront. See our Services page here. No hidden fees or long-term contracts.

Who is this for?

Any small or medium-sized business in Northern BC that wants to save time and serve customers better — retailers, trades, bookkeepers, healthcare offices, restaurants, non-profits, real estate agents, and more. If you're doing repetitive manual tasks that a computer could handle, Ken can probably help.

Do I have to use all your services?

Nope! Every business is unique, so we'll help you find the mix of services that fits just right. Sometimes the usual package works, and sometimes you need something a little more custom. Either way, we'll help you get exactly what you need—nothing more, nothing less.

What areas do you serve?

We focus on Northern BC, including Prince George, Vanderhoof, Fort St. James, Quesnel, and Burns Lake.

What if I need help with my computer, iPhone, or printer?

We specialize in web and business solutions—not tech repair. We do not offer hardware repairs for printers, phones, tablets, laptops, or computers.
