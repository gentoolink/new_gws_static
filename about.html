About Ken McGonigal

I've been in web services long enough to watch search change completely — and I've watched most businesses get left behind every time it does.

When Google launched, businesses that had websites won. When Google started favoring mobile-friendly sites, businesses that adapted won. When local search became a thing, businesses with optimized Google Business Profiles won.

AI search is the next shift. And it's moving faster than any of the ones before it.

Why I built this service

I started noticing something strange: businesses with great Google rankings were invisible in ChatGPT. Not because they were doing anything wrong — because they'd never set up the signals that AI search actually uses.

Structured data. Bing indexing. Content that answers questions clearly. Entity consistency across the web.

These aren't complicated things. But most businesses don't know they matter, and most SEO agencies haven't built a process around them yet.

So I did.
