Local SEO for Trades: Get Found, Get Hired

If you're a contractor, plumber, roofer, or any kind of local service pro...

You already know the phone only rings when people can find you.

And today, "finding you" means one thing:

Showing up on Google when someone nearby needs help.

What Is Local SEO (And Why It Matters)

Local SEO just means:

  • Your business shows up when someone searches for what you do
  • Your phone number, location, hours, and services are easy to find
  • Customers can see real reviews, real photos, and real reasons to trust you

If you're not showing up, you're invisible.
Plain and simple.

Here's How You Win

You don't need magic. You just need a few things dialed in:

  • Google Business Profile fully optimized (this one's huge)
  • Consistent business info across the internet (name, address, phone number)
  • Good reviews coming in steadily (and smart responses to them)
  • Fresh posts and updates that show you're active and open for business

Small stuff — but it makes a massive difference.

Here's the Payoff

When you rank higher locally:

  • More calls

  • More booked jobs

All without spending a dime on ads if you don't want to.
